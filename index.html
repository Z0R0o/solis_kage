Basic Information

  • Name: Solis Kage
  • Definition: Sun-Shadow
  • Race: Human → Legendary Human
  • Class: Shadow Blade Warrior

Origin

Solis Kage was once an ordinary boy constantly bullied in his previous life. After dying during a violent attack, his soul was summoned by a mysterious Dungeon Boss.

Recognizing something unique in his soul, the dungeon granted him a new existence. He became an immortal warrior destined to grow stronger by conquering monsters and absorbing their power.

Main Skills

Essence Forging — Allows Solis to forge weapons using monster cores and dungeon materials.

Eclipse Blade — Sword technique combining light and shadow energy.

Ultimate Skill

Evolution Blood — Solis's blood can evolve monsters that drink it, creating loyal companions.
